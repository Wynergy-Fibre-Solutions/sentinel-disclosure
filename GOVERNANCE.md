\# Governance



This repository is governed as an incubation disclosure policy asset.



\## 1. Claims control



\- No security or compliance guarantees

\- No implied certification

\- Disclosure only applies to declared scope



\## 2. Change control



\- Disclosure rules must remain explicit

\- Additions must not introduce ambiguity

\- Removal preferred over expansion



\## 3. Elevation readiness



Elevation into WFSL requires:



\- adoption in governed repositories

\- deterministic interpretation

\- absence of implied guarantees

\- a tagged elevation baseline



\## 4. Licence discipline



\- Apache License 2.0 only
